What activities has Bethany been involved in?

Turner Dance

Bethany has been dancing since she was 3 years old. She is currently a student and a teacher's assistant at Turner Dance.

Bethany is the most pure in heart person I have ever met. Children are magically drawn to her innate ability to engage their spirits and creativity. Bethany is a very empathetic individual and would do anything in her power to help someone.

- Diane Turner, owner of Turner Dance

St. Vincent de Paul Youth Group

Bethany has been involved in her church's youth group throughout high school. Now that she has graduated, she assists with the program.

Camp Fun 'n Faith

Bethany has attended summer camp at Pro Sanctity's Camp Fun 'n Faith every year since 2011. For the last four years, she has also worked as a junior counselor for the younger kids' day camp. Bethany hopes to continue working as a full counselor in the coming years.

Bethany has been coming to Camp Fun & Faith for many years, first as a camper, and then to assist. She is amazingly generous in her service, cheerful in doing tasks, attentive to what needs to be done, and quick to volunteer for a job.

- Monica, Apostolic Oblate and Camp Director, Pro Sanctity

Steubenville Conference

Every year, Steubenville College in Ohio presents a series of conferences around the country. Bethany has been able to attend for the last three years. She even got to see Matt Maher in concert one of the years!
